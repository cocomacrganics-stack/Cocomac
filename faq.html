Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, ordering, and shipping.

New to Natural Beauty?

What makes Cocomac different from other natural brands?

We're radically transparent. Every ingredient is explained in plain English, every source is verified and traceable, and we never hide behind vague terms like "proprietary blend." Our products have 5-10 ingredients maximum, compared to the 20-40 found in typical "natural" products.

Are natural products as effective as commercial ones?

Often more effective! Commercial products rely on cheap fillers and synthetic ingredients that can irritate skin over time. Our ingredients like baobab oil and grass-fed tallow are biochemically similar to your skin's natural oils, allowing for deeper absorption and nourishment.

How do I choose the right product for my skin?

Start with our Tallow Balm - it works for almost all skin types including dry, oily, and combination. If you're unsure, WhatsApp us at +254 780 980 201 and we'll personally recommend products based on your specific concerns.

About Our Products

Why are your ingredient lists so short?

Because effective skincare doesn't need 40 ingredients. Most commercial products are padded with water, fillers, preservatives, and marketing ingredients. We include only what your skin actually needs - nothing more, nothing less.

How long do your products last?

Unopened products last 12-18 months. Once opened, use within 6 months for optimal freshness. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Our tallow-based products are naturally stable and don't require synthetic preservatives.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our simple formulations are specifically designed to minimize irritation. We avoid common irritants like synthetic fragrances, essential oils in high concentrations, and harsh preservatives. That said, we recommend patch testing any new product.

Can I use these products during pregnancy?

Most of our products are pregnancy-safe as they contain no retinoids, essential oils, or other ingredients typically avoided during pregnancy. However, we always recommend consulting your healthcare provider before using any new skincare during pregnancy.

Ordering & Delivery

How does ordering work?

Add products to your cart, then click Checkout. This opens WhatsApp with your order ready to send. We'll confirm availability, your delivery address, and payment details. You can pay via M-Pesa (preferred) or bank transfer.

How does M-Pesa payment work?

After confirming your order via WhatsApp, we'll send you our M-Pesa details. Once you send payment, share the confirmation message with us. We'll process your order immediately upon verifying payment.

What are shipping costs and delivery times?

Nairobi: KES 200-350, delivered within 1-2 business days.
Other Major Towns: KES 350-500, delivered within 2-4 business days.
Free Shipping: Orders over KES 4,000 qualify for free delivery within Nairobi.

Do you deliver to my area?

We deliver nationwide across Kenya! For locations outside major towns, delivery may take 3-5 business days. WhatsApp us your location and we'll confirm delivery options and costs.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 60-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with a product, contact us within 60 days for a full refund or exchange. Products must be at least 75% unused. We believe in our products enough to offer this guarantee.

Safety & Certification

Are your products KEBS certified?

Yes! All our products are certified by the Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS), meeting national quality and safety requirements. Our certification number is KEBS/CM/2024/00456. View our full certifications on our Transparency page.

How do you test for safety?

Every batch is tested for microbial contamination, stability, and product consistency. We also conduct pH testing and allergen screening. All test results are documented and available upon request.

Where can I see test results?

We're implementing batch traceability where you can enter your product's batch code to see the complete certificate of analysis. This feature launches in Q2 2025. In the meantime, email hello@cocomac.co.ke with your batch code for test results.
