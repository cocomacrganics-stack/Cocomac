Our Story

Why We Started Cocomac

Cocomac Raw Organics was born from a simple observation: the beauty industry often creates more anxiety than confidence. Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, we started with a mission to change that.

Our founders, passionate about natural wellness and frustrated with opaque ingredient lists and exaggerated claims, decided to create products that speak for themselves. We source powerful indigenous ingredients like coconut, macadamia, baobab, shea, and tallow directly from Kenyan communities.

Our Mission

To eliminate the anxiety of choosing wellness products by being radically transparent about what's in our products, where it comes from, and why it works—empowering you to make informed decisions for your body.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash on delivery within Nairobi. Payment details are confirmed via WhatsApp after you place your order.

Nairobi: Same-day or next-day delivery. Rest of Kenya: 2-4 business days. We'll confirm the timeline when you place your order.

Yes! Our products are formulated with minimal ingredients and are free from common irritants. All ingredients are listed for full transparency. If you have specific concerns, reach out to us.
