About Cocomac

Empowering you to make informed decisions for your body with radical transparency and simple, effective natural products.

Our Story

Cocomac Raw Organics was born from a simple observation: the beauty industry often creates more anxiety than confidence. Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, we started with a mission to change that.

Our founders, passionate about natural wellness and frustrated with opaque ingredient lists and exaggerated claims, decided to create products that speak for themselves. We source powerful indigenous ingredients like coconut, macadamia, baobab, shea, and tallow directly from Kenyan communities, ensuring fair partnerships and sustainable practices.

Every product is crafted with care, tested for efficacy, and presented with complete transparency. No hidden agendas, no manufactured needs—just real, effective beauty that nourishes your skin and your peace of mind.

Our Mission

To eliminate the anxiety of choosing wellness products by being radically transparent about what's in our products, where it comes from, and why it works—empowering you to make informed decisions for your body.

We're not here to sell you more products. We're here to sell you better ones.
Fewer products. Clearer choices. Real wellness. Welcome to your safe harbor.
